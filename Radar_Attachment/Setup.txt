Radar Mirror Attachment

Piece One: Radar Rear View Mirror Attachment (Black)
- Made of ABS for standard form rigidity under heat (summer heat of a car). 
- Round clip for securing to the mirror.
- Base feature for securing radar.

Piece Two: Seal (White)
- Made of 95a TPU for extra secure clamping/grip to mirror base. Minor deformation expected due to heat and pressure. 

Piece Three: Base Padding (White)
- Made of 95a TPU for cushion and grip on feature that makes contact with bottom of mirror so the mirror nor mount/radar move during travel.
